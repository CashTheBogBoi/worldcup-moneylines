# Bankroll and Risk Rules

Related app tab: Bankroll Watch

## Core rule

Never put your whole bank account, net worth, rent, savings, emergency fund, or essential money on a pick.

The app's Bankroll Watch tab is a ranked watchlist, not a command.

## Suggested caps

The app labels possible stake sizing as:

- Watch only
- Tiny lean: 0.25%-0.5% bankroll
- Small play: 0.5%-1% bankroll

No pick gets a recommendation above 1%.

## Why

Even positive expected value bets lose often. Long shots can be mathematically interesting and still miss most of the time.

## What makes a pick interesting

- The no-vig market chance is higher than the posted break-even chance.
- The fair line is shorter than the book's posted line.
- The payout is enough to justify the risk.
- The source is fresh.
- The market is not stale, suspended, or already changed.

## What makes a pick dangerous

- You are chasing losses.
- The line moved and the app has stale data.
- The match already started and live context changed.
- You are using no-vig market probability as if it were a personal projection.
- You are betting because the payout looks big, not because the probability beats break-even.
