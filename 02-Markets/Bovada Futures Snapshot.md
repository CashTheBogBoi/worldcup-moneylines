## Sportsbook futures move — 2026-06-30 (live web source, not Bovada)

**Source-labeling note:** this update is from FanDuel Sportsbook, not Bovada — keeping it
separate per [[Source Map]]'s rule not to mix sportsbook sources without labeling them.

- France shortened from +350 to +250 after sweeping the group stage. Part of the move is
  bracket-driven: if France wins its Round of 32 match, the Round of 16 opponent is
  **Paraguay**, not Germany — a softer path than the field expected.
- Argentina held at +400, the clear #2 favorite, after winning Group J and drawing the
  easier side of the knockout bracket. If both favorites hold, the final would be a rematch
  of the 2022 final.
- Source: [FOX Sports World Cup odds](https://www.foxsports.com/stories/soccer/2026-world-cup-odds-spain-france-lead-race-reach-final).

This is directionally consistent with the existing Covers/Kalshi table above (France/Argentina
1-2) but is a sportsbook price, not a prediction-market probability — do not average it with
the Kalshi numbers without converting both to the same basis first (see
[[Probability and Value Math]]).
