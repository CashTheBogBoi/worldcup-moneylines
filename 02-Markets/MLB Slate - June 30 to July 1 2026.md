### Standout matchup: Pirates vs Phillies, July 1

**Paul Skenes vs Zack Wheeler** is the marquee pitching matchup of the window — two of the
better arms in the league. This is the kind of spot [[MLB Research]] flags as needing real
pitcher-quality input rather than a flat strength score: a true ace-vs-ace game often prices
tighter (closer to a coin flip) than a generic team-strength read would suggest, since both
starters suppress scoring relative to their bullpens/offenses. Also notable: **Shohei Ohtani
starts for the Dodgers** against Oakland — both a pitching and an offensive watch given
Ohtani's two-way role.
